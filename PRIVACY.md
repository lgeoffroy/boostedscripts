# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: March 29, 2026*

## Overview

This privacy policy describes how this Firefox extension handles your data. We are committed to being transparent: this extension collects no data on our end, and all data processing happens strictly between your browser and the AI service you configure.

---

## Data We Collect

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any data to our own servers.**

The extension reads content from the current browser tab solely to fulfill its core functionality. This content is sent **directly and exclusively** to the Large Language Model (LLM) provider you configure in the extension settings (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic, a local model, or any other endpoint you specify).

---

## How Your Data Is Used

| Data | Purpose | Stored by us? |
|---|---|---|
| Current page content | Sent to your configured LLM | ❌ No |
| LLM API endpoint / key | Used locally to make requests | ❌ No |

- Page content is read **only when you explicitly trigger** the extension.
- Your API credentials are stored **locally in your browser** using Firefox's built-in storage and are never transmitted anywhere other than the LLM endpoint you configured.

---

## Third-Party Services

This extension communicates with the LLM provider **you choose and configure**. We have no control over how that provider handles the data you send to it. Please review the privacy policy of your chosen LLM provider:

- The provider receives the page content you submit.
- Their data retention, training, and privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

---

## Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:
- Browsing history
- Personal information
- Usage analytics or telemetry
- Crash reports
- Any data whatsoever on our servers

---

## Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect any information from them.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the *Last updated* date above and note the changes in the extension's release notes.
